Program

NGN 2026 scientific program overview

The meeting includes research talks, poster sessions, mentoring interactions, and focused discussion windows across systems, cellular, molecular, cognitive, computational, and translational neuroscience. Final session-wise schedules will be published after abstract review.

Talks and Posters

Presentation format and abstract notes

  • Participants can select Talk Presentation or Poster Presentation during registration.
  • Talk submissions will be reviewed before final scheduling in oral presentation sessions.
  • Poster dimensions: 4 x 3 ft landscape (Width: 122 cm max, Height: 92 cm max).
  • Session-wise oral and poster rosters will be announced after registration and abstract screening.
