What Registration Includes

Important notes

  • Food will be provided for all registered participants.
  • Accommodation support will be provided for registered participants.
  • Registration may close before the deadline if available slots are filled.
Participation

Who should register

  • Students, postdoctoral fellows, faculty members, and interdisciplinary neuroscience researchers.
  • Participants interested in presenting either a talk or a poster.
  • Attendees who want access to conference sessions, networking, and discussion forums.
Abstract Submission

Talk and poster submission rules

  • The registration form provides two presentation choices: Talk Presentation or Poster Presentation.
  • Any participant can choose either option in the form based on their preference; there is no category-wise restriction.
  • Talk submissions will be reviewed before final scheduling in oral presentation sessions.
  • Poster dimensions: 4 x 3 ft landscape (Width: 122 cm max, Height: 92 cm max).
